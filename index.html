About Me

Hi, I'm Mohammad Danish Hakim bin Mohd Nasir, a Computer Science student specializing in Network and Security at International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM) in Gombak, Selangor.

I've been programming since I was 10 years old, with strongest expertise in JavaScript. Recently, I've been focusing on cybersecurity, participating in CTFs including wargames.my (2023-2024) and UMCS CTF 2025, with particular interest in Web Exploitation.

Beyond programming, I have digital editing skills in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, experience in live streaming with equipment like ATEM Mini Pro and OBS Studio, and a background in robotics from representing my school in competitions for 3 years.
